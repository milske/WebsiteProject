Places to visit in Barcelona

In these pictures you can find some corners of Barcelona. The places one should visit at least include Sagrada Familia, Arc de Triomf, the Barceloneta beach, Los bunkers for the view, Park Guell and roam the streets to check out the interesting buildings of Gaudi which you can find mostly in Passeig de Gracia. If you go to Tibidabo you also get a nice view of the city and for the kids, there is a nice amusement park. In Montjuic you can visit the museum of the national art of Catalunya or see the fountain show in the evening.
