The food in Spain is delicous, focusing on seafood, ham, olives, tapas and so on. Some of the best paellas in Barcelona you can try in a restaurant called La Mar Salada, "the salty sea". The restaurant is right in the center of the city, in the Barceloneta neighbourhood, which is close to the beach and the harbour. On a sunny day, after you have tried some seafood or maybe some tapas, enjoy a vermut with olives on a sunny terrace, a tipical Sunday passtime of the locals.

Or maybe you want to skip the lunch and go directly for the dessert, in which case, buy some churros in the Gothic neighbourhood, or maybe enter the La Boqueria food market found on the La Rambla. There you can find all sorts of sweets, as well as fresh fruits, meat, fish and whatnot. Just enter with patience as the place gets quite full specially during the summer months being very popular among the tourist.

There are so many good restaurants to choose from and so many different cuisines so be prepared to eat well. One of the places one should try is a restaurant called Mont Bar. The place offers variety of "tapas" made of ingredients and combinations from all around the world. Or maybe try a another excellent tapas place El Vaso de Oro, "the golden glass", to have some Patatas Bravas, croquettes or cuttlefish. Be prepared to shout your order though as the volume in the tapas bar can get quite high.
